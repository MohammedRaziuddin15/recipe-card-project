Hyderabadi Biryani

Preparation Time: 45 Minutes

Serves: 4 People

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Basmati Rice
  • 500g Mutton
  • 3 Onions
  • Yogurt
  • Mutton Masala

Cooking Steps

  1. Wash the rice.
  2. Marinate the Mutton.
  3. Cook the rice.
  4. Cook the Mutton.
  5. Layer and dum cook.
